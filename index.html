About

I recently graduated from Cornell with a Bachelor’s degree in Information Science (Behavioral Science, Data Science, and UX). I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction at UMD.

As an aspiring UX designer with a solid foundation in front-end development and data visualization, I am fascinated by the design and technical aspects behind the user experience. By bridging the gap between the two, I design user-centered solutions while keeping technical constraints in mind and collaborate effectively with developers to smooth the implementation process.

I aim to improve digital accessibility through my work by sharing modern technology with as many people as possible, giving them the tools to solve real world problems.

In my free time, you will find me reading, watching Korean dramas, or feeding my sourdough starter. ✌️
