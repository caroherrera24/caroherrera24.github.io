About

I recently graduated from Cornell with a Bachelor’s degree in Information Science (Behavioral Science, Data Science, and UX). I am currently pursing a Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction at UMD.

As an aspiring UX designer with a solid foundation in front-end development and data visualization, I am fascinated by both the design and technical aspects behind the user experience. By bridging the gap between the two, I design flexible, user-centered solutions while keeping technical constraints in mind and work with developers to ensure a smooth implementation process.

I aim to enhance digital accessibility through my work to share modern technological tools with as many people as possible, helping them solve real world problems.

In my free time, you will find me reading, watching Korean dramas, or feeding my sourdough starter.
